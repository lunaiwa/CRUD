What is Athlete Appetite ?

Athlete Appetite is a website for busy athletes to quickly determine what nutritious meal they should make next. Considering the individual's desired cuisine, personal diet, and time available to make food, we provide personalized recommendations to satisfy their hunger. A picture of their potential meal, equipment, ingredients, and nutrition details will all be outputted for full transparency and easy access!





Our Mission

Many student athletes find it really hard to make time in our schedule to plan and make food that we know is beneficial to our body and mind. We have heard many of our peers complain about being malnourished and not having the motivation to start up cooking/baking, so we want to try to provide a super simple and easy to use tool that can help get them started.
